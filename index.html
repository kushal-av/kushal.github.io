Welcome to the World of Trees

Introduction

Trees are essential to life on Earth. They provide oxygen, store carbon, stabilize the soil, and give life to countless species.

Types of Trees

  • Deciduous Trees (e.g., Oak, Maple)
  • Evergreen Trees (e.g., Pine, Spruce)
  • Tropical Trees (e.g., Banyan, Coconut)

Why Trees Matter

Trees help combat climate change, reduce air pollution, and provide shade and beauty to our surroundings. Planting trees is one of the simplest ways to make a positive impact on the planet.
